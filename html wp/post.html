The DONATE platform ensures anyone with a mobile phone can give immediately and securely to the causes they care about.

DONATE is different to other providers because we:

  • focus on mobile giving
  • are a charity
  • enable charities to promote multiple campaigns simultaneously
  • provide an integrated text and mobile-web donation/payment platform
  • offer an integrated silent auction platform
  • provide innovative text raffle solutions

DONATE PLATINUM

DONATE Platinum is a set of extra services to maximise fundraising before, during and after your event. DONATE Platinum provides you with the complete fundraising service:

“Adding a silent auction platform to the existing platform provides charitable organisations with a one-stop shop. DONATE now offers an integrated fundraising solution covering text giving, text-raffles, web-app donations and now a feature-rich, silent auction capability. This major extension to DONATE continues to deliver against our objective to ensure charities have access to the most cost-efficient way of fundraising from their supporters”. - Philippa Esson, Development & Communications Director
