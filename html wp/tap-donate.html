tap+DONATE

How it works

Step 1

Order or rent your tap+DONATE product

Step 2

Donors tap their card or phone

Step 3

Charities receive donations

At long last – a cost efficient rental or purchase scheme for contactless giving to charity.

Contactless giving has been much talked about but is now a reality. Order your tap+DONATE product today, apply branding stickers and you will be collecting cash-free donations tomorrow. We provide the device, fund collection and banking leaving you to raise funds.

FAQs

Can any organisation use the tap+DONATE service?

Tap+DONATE is available to any charitable organisation that is registered with DONATE. If you are not yet registered with DONATE you can do so for free here

What does the product look like

Please see these images;

Unit with lid closed, and the unit with lid open.

Can I either rent or buy the service?

Yes, we provide both a rental and purchase service. Please download and send this form to place your request. Units are normally sent within 7-10 days following a confirmed order. Please note you must be registered with DONATE to place an order.

Can I convert a device rental into a purchase of the device?

Yes, following the 3 month minimum rental the device can be purchased for a one-off fee of £150+VAT

Can the device be secured to a table?

Yes, screw holes are provided to secure the device to a table/wall. However, we only recommend this in specific, secure locations. Also note that experience shows that donations are more likely when there is a conversation with a donor.

How are funds transferred to our charity?

Funds are received by National Funding Scheme (registered charity) and passed across (less fees) as per our Affiliation Agreement (see Agreements for charities and non-charities).

What are the fees for rental?

There is a one-off set-up fee (per organisation) of £350 and a monthly rental of £45 with a minimum term of 3 months. Renting organisations will also need to make a returnable deposit of £250 per device and are responsible for insurance of the products.

A per donation charge of 4.5% is made per donation (charities), 5% for approved non-charities.

All fees are exclusive of VAT.

What are the fees to purchase a device?

There is a one-off set-up fee (per organisation) of £350 and a purchase fee of £350 per device.

A per donation charge of 4.5% is made per donation (charities), 5% for approved non-charities.

All fees are exclusive of VAT.

Can tap+DONATE donations be Gift Aided?

Gift Aid is not available as no personal data per donor is asked for/recorded.

What do we need to provide to enable the device to work?

Tap+DONATE devices require a Bluetooth connected device (tablet/phone) with an internet (WiFi or 3G/4G) to the internet.

What comes with the tap+DONATE product?

We provide:

  • Access to an app (that you will need to load onto the internet connected device)
  • Training materials
  • Contactless pin-pad and tap+DONATE forma
  • Marketing template for stickers to be attached to the forma
  • Spare battery
  • Lanyard (so unit can be hung from neck)

How is the donation level set (regular and one-off amounts)?

The provided app allows your staff/volunteers to set a regular donation amount and interrupt this for taking larger one-off payments.

A chip/PIN facility enables transactions above the contactless ceiling of £30.

How do we apply our branding to the loaned or purchased device?

We provide a template showing where your branding can be applied. Once artworked according to the template you should have stickers printed using low-tack material (for easy peeling).

Can donation refunds be offered?

Your staff/volunteers will, be default, be able to provide a refund to a donor (this facility can be turned off if requested). Please note that, as per the terms of our Affiliation Agreement, National Funding Scheme will not offer refunds.
