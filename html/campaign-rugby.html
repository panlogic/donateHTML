The Rugby Business Network is the main connector of rugby and business in the world. Life as a professional rugby player is temporary and can end as quickly as it started. Therefore it is extremely important that there is a plan in place to prepare for a transition into a successful post rugby business career. The Life After Rugby Programme works closely with business people with a passion for rugby and rugby players with a passion for business to prepare players for their future in business

The programme includes:

  • Improving players online profile, including LinkedIn training, CV assistance and emotional intelligence awareness
  • A structured player work placement
  • Helping players to set up businesses of their own
  • Introductions to business leaders and decision makers through the RBN's extensive worldwide network.

There are many similarities between rugby and business. Vital skills such as effective communication, relationship management, competitiveness and teamwork are all completely transferable from the rugby field. Skills that will add value and benefit to any business. It is this rationale, which forms the basis for the Life After Rugby Programme.
